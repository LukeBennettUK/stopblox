StopBlox is the most effective on-going solution to kitchen drain problems

What is StopBlox

StopBlox is an effective on-going drain treatment, the most effective available & 100% natural;

No soaps or harsh chemicals, no premixing and no waiting.

StopBlox is a friendly bacteria that quite literally eats-up fat, oil and grease (FOG) - converting it into carbon dioxide and water: eliminating costly grease-related problems which cause slow and blocked drains.

Unlike other products, StopBlox will not emulsify fats, oils and grease. Therefore trapped grease levels are reduced at point of build-up and not transferred downstream, reducing the incidence of blockages and the need for grease trap pumping

How does StopBlox Work?

How does StopBlox Work?

As soon as StopBlox is in the drain, billions of microscopic live bacteria begin to work, feeding and digesting on the organic build-up in the pipes - multiplying and growing in the food rich environment.

StopBox is a live bacteria that once placed in the wastewater system, can produce the type of enzyme needed for specialised degration, the bacteria will continue to produce these enzymes as long as there is food available.

By using bacteria, we are in essence putting enzyme "factories" into the wastewater, not just the enzymes themselves.

That is the StopBlox difference.

Why use StopBlox?

Why use StopBlox?

It is easier and cheaper to maintain drains rather than have them unblocked, or replaced entirely.

Until now, to achieve effective maintenance of drains has required consistent application of maintenance chemicals such as strong acids or alkali's and other hazardous chemicals.

StopBlox will:

Eliminate foul odours caused by rancid grease

Improve hygiene, helping prevent rodent & insect infestation

Reduce grease trap maintenance
